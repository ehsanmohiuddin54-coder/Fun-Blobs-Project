⚖️ Shapes, Weights & Colors

Not all blobs weigh the same — and that's what makes Fun Blobs such a satisfying brain game. Learning each shape's weight is the key to mastering the balance and achieving a high score.

Shape Weight Colour Strategy Tip
🟠 Circle1 pointOrange Lightest — ideal for fine-tuning balance with satisfying precision

💗 Triangle3 pointsPink Heaviest and most valuable — handle with care to avoid tipping

🌟 Pro Tips for a High Score

  • Prioritise triangles wisely. Each pink triangle is worth 3 points — the fastest way to grow your score. Always plan where to send them before you drag.
  • Watch the warning countdown. When the balance tip turns yellow, act immediately — drag the nearest blob to the lighter side.
  • Use circles to fine-tune. Orange circles weigh only 1 point, perfect for correcting a slight imbalance without overcompensating.
  • Look ahead. Keep one eye on grounded blobs — reshuffling one can save you when the balance tips unexpectedly.
  • Stay calm at higher levels. From Level 2 onwards blobs spawn faster. Breathe, focus on balance first, score second.
  • Build your combo. Staying balanced for 5 consecutive seconds builds your combo multiplier — a reward for inner calm.

💡 Levels, Combos & Progression

Level 1 (0–14 weight) is slow and meditative. Level 2 (15–29) introduces a slightly busier blob stream. Level 3 (30+) flows at a satisfying, rewarding pace. Progression is gradual and never punishing.
